## The problem worth solving

A global medical-imaging, film and dental capital-equipment manufacturer ran service across regulated clinical environments, where a field visit has to satisfy safety, quality and compliance standards that most industries never encounter. Sales, service and field operations were splintered across regions and systems.

## What was built

Venkata pioneered the global architecture and rollout of **SAP Cloud for Customer (C4C) and Field Service Management**, establishing one unified service core for complex imaging, film and high-precision dental capital systems. End-to-end service processes integrated C4C, FSM and ECC for seamless service-order execution, parts tracking and technician enablement.

The FSM mobile solution was engineered for the field reality: intelligent resource-scheduling optimisation, real-time GPS routing and **offline handling**, all aligned to the stringent safety, quality and regulatory compliance of clinical settings. Data integration between the front-end CX systems and backend ECC ran over CPI and Cloud Connector, keeping equipment master, installed base and historical service records in absolute sync. A MindTouch Knowledge Central integration brought film, dental and medical know-how into the C4C service and self-service portal.

## Why it matters

The Human layer is hardest where the stakes are highest. Designing service journeys that hold up under clinical regulation — and giving technicians a mobile tool that works offline in a hospital basement — is CX as an engineering discipline, not a coat of paint.
