## The problem worth solving

Two industrial manufacturers — one in building products and commercial roofing, one in rail and infrastructure — needed sales, service and field operations to run off the same contract reality. On-site repairs, factory refurbishments and preventive field calls were being priced by hand, and margin leaked wherever the contract terms lived in a different system from the service ticket.

## What was built

Venkata delivered multi-site SAP **Sales Cloud V2, Service Cloud V2 and CPQ** across both manufacturers. Service Cloud V2 ticketing was configured to pull complex project- and contract-based pricing matrices straight from **S/4HANA and ECC into FSM**, so margins calculate automatically for on-site repairs, refurbishments and preventive maintenance — no spreadsheets, no leakage.

Guided selling in CPQ carried recommendation logic for heavy-equipment bundling, spare-parts cross-sell and dynamic SLA assignment. Architecture governance was run properly: business capability maps, master blueprints and design standards, with Signavio used to model and audit the end-to-end value streams. On the LB Foster estate the footprint spanned 15 business units, 33 sales areas and 28 quote-to-cash process areas across a single integrated design.

## Why it matters

Contract-driven field operations are where CX and SAP meet the P&L. Getting the pricing to follow the ticket — automatically — is the difference between a service business that protects margin and one that quietly gives it away.
