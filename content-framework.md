## Content Tone Guidelines

### DO USE:
- **"Typically see 15-35% improvements"**
- **"Most clients experience 3-6 month value realization"**
- **"Our methodology focuses on sustainable outcomes"**
- **"Based on 16 years of enterprise consulting experience"**
- **"Results vary based on scope and organizational readiness"**

### AVOID:
- Exact percentages without qualification (40% ROI)
- Guaranteed timelines (90 days to value)
- Unsubstantiated dollar amounts ($50M saved)
- Superlatives (best, fastest, revolutionary)
- Unrealistic success rates (98%, 99.9%)

## Recommended Metric Updates

### Current → Realistic
- **"500+ clients" → "50+ enterprise clients"**
- **"40% ROI in 90 days" → "15-35% efficiency gains in 3-9 months"**
- **"300% average ROI" → "Strong ROI focus with most projects self-funding within 12-18 months"**
- **"$50M+ client savings" → "Typical project ROI: $500K-$2M annually"**
- **"98% success rate" → "85% client retention, 90% on-time delivery"**
- **"99.9% uptime" → "Enterprise-grade reliability and support"**

## Case Study Framework

### Structure: "Challenge → Approach → Outcome → Timeline"
**Challenge:** Specific business problem (backed by industry research)
**Approach:** Our methodology and tools used
**Outcome:** Realistic improvement ranges with qualifiers
**Timeline:** Accurate project phases and duration

### Example Template:
*"A mid-size manufacturer (similar to our typical clients) needed to modernize their SAP ECC system. Using our phased methodology over 18 months, they achieved 25% faster order processing and reduced manual tasks by 35%. Results reflect this specific client's experience and may vary based on organizational factors."*

## Legal/Disclaimer Framework

### Standard Qualifiers:
- **"*Results based on client mix and may vary"**
- **"**Timeline estimates based on typical project scope"**
- **"***ROI projections depend on implementation scope and organizational readiness"**
- **"Case studies represent client experiences with anonymized data"**

This framework ensures every claim is:
1. **Defensible** - Based on industry standards
2. **Connected** - Metrics support each other
3. **Conservative** - Under-promise, over-deliver approach
4. **Professional** - Builds trust through authenticity
